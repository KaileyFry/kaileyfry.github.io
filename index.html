Kailey Draws

I am Kailey Fry, MFA. I love to draw, especially things that are important to you. I initially got my start in doing commissioned work drawing portraits of diabetic alert service dogs for my fellow Type 1 diabetics. Since then, I have taken on commissions for logos, landscapes, pets in general, and educational posters for children and animal enthusiasts.

While my academic background is in photography and collaborative design, I've always enjoyed drawing and painting. My process for commissioned work is digital, but I am happy to be able to draw on my years of experience as a photographer to make sure that prints come out looking exactly as vibrant and beautiful as the works deserve.
